The Importance

Wildlife like this elephant needs our protection to survive and thrive.

Sentience

Animals feel pain, joy, fear, and love. Recognizing their capacity to suffer compels us to treat them with compassion and respect.

Ecology

Every species plays a vital role in ecosystems. Protecting animals helps maintain biodiversity and a healthy planet for all.

Ethics

As conscious beings with power over other species, we have a moral responsibility to prevent suffering and uphold dignity.
